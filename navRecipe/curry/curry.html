Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 cups vegetables (e.g., potatoes, carrots, peas)
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add the chopped onions, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until the onions are translucent.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and release their juices.
  4. Stir in the curry powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook for a minute to toast the spices.
  5. Add the vegetables and mix well. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a simmer. Cook until the vegetables are tender and the sauce has thickened.
  7. Stir in the garam masala and cook for another 2 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.
