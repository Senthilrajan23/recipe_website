Naan Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons oil or melted butter
  • 1-2 tablespoons ghee or melted butter (for brushing)
  • Minced garlic or chopped cilantro (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the sugar in warm milk and sprinkle the yeast over it. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes, until frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Make a well in the center and add the yogurt, yeast mixture, and oil or melted butter. Mix until a soft dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes, until smooth and elastic. Place it in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours, or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat a tawa or non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into an oval or tear-shaped naan, about 1/4-inch thick.
  7. Place the rolled-out naan onto the hot tawa. Cook for about 1-2 minutes, or until bubbles start to form. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  8. If desired, brush the naan with ghee or melted butter and sprinkle with minced garlic or chopped cilantro.
  9. Remove from the skillet and keep warm in a cloth-lined container. Repeat with the remaining dough balls.
  10. Serve warm with your favorite curry or vegetable dish.
