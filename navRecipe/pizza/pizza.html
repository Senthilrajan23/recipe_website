Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add the pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, and mushrooms.
  6. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven, slice, and serve hot.
