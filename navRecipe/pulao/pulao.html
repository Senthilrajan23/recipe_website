Veg Pulao Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, etc.), chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Fried onions for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak it in water for 20-30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a large pan or pressure cooker, heat oil or ghee over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds, and let them splutter.
  3. Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Add the ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and the oil starts to separate from the masala.
  5. Mix in the turmeric powder, garam masala, coriander powder, chili powder, and salt. Stir well.
  6. Add the chopped mixed vegetables and sauté for a few minutes. Stir in the yogurt and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Add the drained rice and mix gently. Pour in 2 cups of water and bring to a boil.
  8. Reduce heat to low, cover the pan, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  9. Once done, remove from heat and let it sit covered for 5 minutes. Fluff the rice with a fork.
  10. Garnish with fresh cilantro and fried onions if desired. Serve hot with raita or a side salad.
