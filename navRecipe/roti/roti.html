Roti Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup water (approximately)
  • 1-2 teaspoons oil or ghee (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the whole wheat flour and salt. Gradually add water, a little at a time, and knead the dough until it is soft and pliable.
  2. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 15-30 minutes.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls of equal size.
  4. On a floured surface, roll each ball into a thin circle, about 6-8 inches in diameter.
  5. Heat a tawa or non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Place the rolled-out dough onto the hot tawa.
  6. Cook for about 30 seconds, or until you see bubbles forming on the surface. Flip the roti and cook the other side for another 30 seconds.
  7. Press gently with a clean cloth or spatula to encourage puffing. Flip again and cook for a few more seconds if needed.
  8. Remove from the skillet and place in a container lined with a cloth to keep warm and soft. Repeat with the remaining dough balls.
  9. Serve hot with your favorite curry or vegetable dish.
