Samosa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water (as needed)
  • 2 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup peas, boiled
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons cilantro, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, salt, and vegetable oil. Gradually add water and knead to form a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. For the filling, heat some oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds, and let them splutter.
  3. Add the chopped ginger and saut√© for a minute. Add turmeric powder, garam masala, chili powder, and salt.
  4. Add the mashed potatoes, peas, and cilantro. Mix well and cook for a few minutes. Allow the filling to cool.
  5. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each ball into a thin circle. Cut the circle in half to make two semi-circles.
  6. Form a cone shape with each semi-circle, fill with the potato mixture, and seal the edges to form a triangle.
  7. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the samosas until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with chutney.
